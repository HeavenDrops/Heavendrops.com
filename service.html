OUR SERVICES

Water purification

Involves the process of removing contaminants and impurities from water, making it safe for human consumption. There are various methods of water purification, including filtration, sedimentation, and disinfection, among others.

Water distribution

The process of supplying clean and safe water to communities, households, and businesses through a network of pipelines, pumps, and other infrastructure. Water distribution systems ensure that people have access to water when they need it, whether it's for drinking, cooking, cleaning, or other purposes.

Water quality testing

Involves analyzing water samples to determine their chemical, physical, and biological characteristics. Testing helps identify potential contaminants and other issues that may affect the safety and quality of the water. It's essential for ensuring that people have access to water that meets regulatory standards and is safe to drink.

Water treatment

Involves the process of removing contaminants and impurities from water using various methods such as chemical treatment, sedimentation, and filtration. Treatment is necessary to ensure that water meets regulatory standards and is safe for human consumption.

Water Refilling

Involves refilling water containers, such as bottles or jugs, with clean and safe drinking water. This service is often provided through vending machines, kiosks, or other distribution points and is an essential service in areas where access to clean drinking water may be limited.
